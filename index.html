Biography

Sarder Fakhrul Abedin received his B.S. degree in Computer Science from Kristianstad University, Kristianstad, Sweden, in 2013. He received his Ph.D. degree in Computer Engineering from Kyung Hee University, South Korea in 2020. His research interests include Internet of Things (IoT) network management, Cloud computing, Fog computing, and Wireless sensor network. Mr. Abedin is a Member of the KIISE.
